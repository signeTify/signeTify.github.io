Tengis Dorj

About


Tengis has worked with film and digital alike. Starting off in his highschool darkroom, developing and printing 35mm stills. Where he decided to pursue Cinematography as a career.

Graduating in 2021 after studying the Bachelor of Film and Television (Honours) at Swinburne University of Technology. Tengis has had experience in many aspects within the filmmaking process from AC'ing to gaffing to of course as DOP.

Since then he has been shooting a variety of Music Videos and Commercials. Always looking to achieve visually striking pieces of work. As well as working as a Prep Tech at Panavision Melbourne.
